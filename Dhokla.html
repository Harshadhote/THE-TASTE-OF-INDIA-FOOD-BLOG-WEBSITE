Dhokla Recipe

Enjoy the soft and fluffy Gujarati delicacy, Dhokla, with this simple recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 tablespoon semolina (rava)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-green chili paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon Eno fruit salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Batter: In a mixing bowl, combine besan, semolina, ginger-green chili paste, turmeric powder, lemon juice, sugar, and salt. Gradually add water and whisk to form a smooth batter.
  2. Add Eno: Just before steaming, add Eno fruit salt to the batter and mix well. The batter will become frothy and light.
  3. Steam Dhokla: Pour the batter into a greased steaming dish. Steam in a steamer or pressure cooker (without the whistle) for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  4. Prepare Tempering: Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add sesame seeds and let them brown slightly.
  5. Finish Dhokla: Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla. Garnish with chopped cilantro and grated coconut. Cut into squares and serve warm with green chutney.
