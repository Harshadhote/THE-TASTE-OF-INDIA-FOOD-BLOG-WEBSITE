Rajma Chawal Recipe

Enjoy a comforting and delicious Rajma Chawal with this easy-to-follow recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kidney beans (rajma), soaked overnight
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • 1.5 cups basmati rice, cooked

Instructions:

  1. Cook Rajma: Drain soaked rajma and pressure cook with 2 cups of water for about 15-20 minutes until soft. Set aside.
  2. Prepare Masala: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add Spices: Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute. Add turmeric, coriander, garam masala, and red chili powder. Mix well.
  4. Cook Tomatoes: Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Combine Rajma: Add the cooked rajma (along with its water) to the masala. Season with salt. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  6. Serve: Serve hot rajma over a bed of cooked basmati rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
