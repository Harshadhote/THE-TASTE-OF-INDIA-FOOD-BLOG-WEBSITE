Idli Recipe

Enjoy the soft and fluffy South Indian delicacy, Idli, with this easy recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak Ingredients: Soak urad dal and fenugreek seeds together in water for 4-6 hours. Soak idli rice separately for the same amount of time.
  2. Grind Batter: Drain the soaked urad dal and grind it to a smooth paste, adding water as needed. Drain and grind the rice to a slightly coarse paste. Mix both pastes together, add salt, and mix well. The batter should have a pouring consistency.
  3. Ferment Batter: Allow the batter to ferment overnight or for about 8-12 hours in a warm place until it doubles in volume.
  4. Steam Idlis: Grease idli molds and pour the batter into them. Steam for 10-15 minutes until the idlis are cooked and fluffy. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Dosa Recipe

Enjoy crispy and delicious Dosas with this simple recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups dosa rice
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak Ingredients: Soak urad dal and fenugreek seeds together in water for 4-6 hours. Soak dosa rice separately for the same amount of time.
  2. Grind Batter: Drain the soaked urad dal and grind it to a smooth paste, adding water as needed. Drain and grind the rice to a slightly coarse paste. Mix both pastes together, add salt, and mix well. The batter should have a pouring consistency.
  3. Ferment Batter: Allow the batter to ferment overnight or for about 8-12 hours in a warm place until it doubles in volume.
  4. Cook Dosas: Heat a non-stick pan or tawa. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the center and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin crepe. Drizzle a little oil around the edges. Cook until the edges start to lift and the dosa is golden and crispy. Flip and cook the other side if desired. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
